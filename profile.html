    Here are a few steps involved in the film developing process:
  1. Load the film negatives from the film canister to the developing tank (in my case I use a paterson tank and a changing bag to reduce the need for a darkroom

  2. Process the film negatives inside the tank, via a mixture of chemicals around 95°-105°

  3. Transfer the film to a clean, cool area to dry. Try to avoid areas with high traffic to minimize any dust settling on the film.
